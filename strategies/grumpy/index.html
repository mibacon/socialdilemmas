Grumpy

Starts off cooperating, but with each defection by partner, Grumpy's "grumpiness" counter increases. Once a certain "mean" threshold is hit, (in this case, 5 points), Grumpy begins to defect. Grumpy will resume cooperating once a "nice" threshold is met (in this case -5).
