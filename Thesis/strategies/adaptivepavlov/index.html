Adaptive Pavlov 2011

Tries to categorize partner into one of four groups: Cooperator, Defector, Suspicious Tit for Tat (a variant of Tit for Tat where strategy starts off defecting instead of cooperating) or Random. Then bases decisions on trying to elicit mutual cooperation, but will defect on uncooperative partners.
